In the heart of Europe lies a city responsible for so much bad but also good, a people who have struggled but also made others struggle too.

Dated back to the 13th century, Berlin is the Capital of Germany, a city renowned for its historical buildings, history and modern art. It is the place to be for any aspiring artist interested in the modern art scene.

The post war state of the country made way for modern advancement and ideas in the realm of architecture which paved the way for ideas like Brutalism and Minimalism, Berlin is a center for a modern and proggresive views concerning topics such as politics, globar warming and inclusive rights for Lgbtq and ethnic populations.

Plagued by the guilt of their past, Germany and the German people are dead set on changing the world for the better, by introducing new ideas for buildings, rights for minorities or a proggresive geo-political stance, Berlin is certainly looking towards the future.
