German Food

You have all heard of German food, whether you wanted to or not you probably have also eaten something that has had its origin in Germany. There is a good reason why Germany's food has spread as far around the world as it has, which is because it’s fatty, rich and unhealthy but incredibly tasty most of the time.

Some of their most commonly recognizable dishes include the Bratwürst a sausage with spices, the pretzel a piece of slender bread shaped like a loose knot and of course the authentic German beer served in a stein.
